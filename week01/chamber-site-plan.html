Site Name: York Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

York is the city I am most familiar with, being my home.

Purpose: The York Chamber of Commerce operates to enhance the York area. We aim to invite new economic opportunity, attract strong leadership, and foster a true sense of community among the residents. We promote local businesses, highlight recent developments in the area, and provide guidance to lead the region's growth.

Scenarios:

  • What does the chamber actively do for the community?
  • What has the chamber done in the past?
  • What is the growth history of the area, both in population and business?
  • How can I contact the chamber?
  • How can I apply to join the chamber?

Color Schema:

  • #556b2f (dark olive green) for headers and highlights.
  • #f5f5f5 (white smoke) for text on headers and highlights.
  • #fafad2 (light goldenrod) for body sections.
  • #000000 (black) for normal text.

Typography:

  • Nunito for normal text.
